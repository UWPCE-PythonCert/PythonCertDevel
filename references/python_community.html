Python Community¶

Having signed up for a course with us, you’re taking your first steps down the road to becoming a world-class Python programmer!

When you’re first starting out in a new field or community, it’s often good to seek out people already leading within that community to get a feel for what they’ve done to succeed within it. With that in mind, here are some suggested people and resources you can follow to become more familiar with this particular tech circle.

Make sure your new projects are in Python 3!
